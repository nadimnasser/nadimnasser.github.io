    Hafeez Ladha

    for Don Valley East.

    I’m seeking the Liberal party nomination to ensure the communities in Don Valley East have strong, active, experienced and values-driven representation in Ottawa.






    Biography

    Hafeez Ladha

    Social Entrepreneur and Public Health Advocate


    I’m a second generation Canadian who was born in the Thorncliffe Park neighbourhood and grew up in Markham in a family of South Asian and East African origin. I feel truly lucky to have grown up in Canada, and in dynamic and multicultural communities across the GTA. I have spent my career fighting for access to healthcare for underserved populations, working to make medicines and critical health commodities more affordable to communities (with the Clinton Health Access Initiative), building economic opportunity and jobs in areas that need them the most across Sub-Saharan Africa and Asia (with the World Bank) and helping support the growth of companies across the US and Canada (with the management consulting firm, Bain & Company). I have first-hand experience working to create the economic and social conditions that help businesses and communities succeed. I will bring this experience and knowledge to help our community thrive.






    Why I'm Running


    There are a number of challenges facing our community today – ensuring affordable housing for our families and seniors; equipping our healthcare system to serve our families while being accessible to all when they need it; continuing to provide support to new immigrants to ensure they are able to thrive and contribute to our economy and communities; building a Canada that continues to be the inclusive, progressive, safe and economically productive country we love. I want to ensure the voices of all Don Valley East residents are heard as we keep our Liberal government in Ottawa over the coming years.





    What Does An MP Do?

    Voice

    Represent you to the federal government, voicing your needs, concerns and perspectives on issues of that are important to you.

    Law

    Inform creation of laws and policies on issues you care about, from increasing the accessibility of healthcare to ensuring the availability of sufficient high-quality jobs to investing in addressing climate change.

    Support

    Help you access support from the federal government on important issues such as unemployment insurance, immigration, legal challenges and more.

    My Core Beliefs

    Skill training

    I support investments in training for Canadians who are seeking to grow in their careers, pivot careers or are just starting out.

    Support for new Canadians

    I commit to working with the city and provincial government to ensure the housing needs of our residents are addressed.

    Economic Development

    I will support the city’s plan to grow jobs in the region and ultimately put more income in the hands of its residents.

    Social Assistance

    I support the expansion of a universal basic income pilot to explore a model that provides Canadians the financial latitude to invest in their future.

    Pharmacare

    I support the development of a National Pharmacare strategy, initially prioritizing coverage for our children, seniors and those most vulnerable in our families and communities.


    Early childhood support

    I support federal financial contributions to families to ensure all children have access to holistic early learning and development opportunities.


    Caring for our seniors

    I support greater investment in home health services and affordable senior living communities that are accessible and culturally relevant.

    Housing

    I commit to working with newly immigrated families to provide support to ensure they are able to live happy, successful and productive lives in their new community.
